About us

We are Wanderers… seeking, reaching out to quench our thirst for friendship… for companionship. To be alone and to be in the midst; to be free and to be ensconced; to be warm and to be set to elements!

……and…. We are Craft Brewers of beer!

(Wo)Man has been wandering for ages, hunting (in search of food & drink), for business (to meet and greet), to find solace and to find oneself too!

We are Wanderers, set out to seek and meet fellow wanderers in our journey. We are travellers and hosts at once; we serve our fellow travellers walking in; travelling with us, striving to enhance their social bonding, savouring our craft beer and artisan food.

Our Muse ~ Arctic Terns

Arctic Tern ((Sterna paradisaea), a strongly migrating bird which has a circumpolar breeding distribution covering Arctic, sub-Arctic and Antarctic coast, travelling over 70-90,000 Km EVERY YEAR, is our Wanderer-in-Chief!

They are by far the longest known migrating species known, seeing two summers each year, get to experience more daylight than any other animal in the world (hence ‘Bird of the Sun’) as it migrates from its northern Arctic breeding grounds to southern Antarctic coast for summer and back again each year. They are estimated to travel over 90,000 Km every year, travelling over 2.4 million KM,which is three trips to the Moon!

Sustainability

Business and environment sustainability is ingrained in our business

We are, probably, the only microbrewery in India with an in-house ETP (Effluent Treatment Plant) confirming to the norms of the effluent discharge.

We diligently adhere to the 3Rs - Reduce, Reuse & Recycle - in our supply chain and operations. We try to bring down our water usage by:

  • Conserving water.
  • Reusing treated water within our brew-pub thereby reducing our water requirement and footprint.
  • Recycling our food and non-food waste creating a backward integration of supply chain.
