Our Beers

With the state-of-the-art brewery from Germany, from one of the oldest manufacturers of brew-houses in the world, our brewery is our commitment to our beers! Brewed with malts from maltsters who are equally passionate about their science & art of malting; brewed by our brewers with passion, commitment and life dedicated to beers; our beers are a continuation of the tradition in the Art & Pride in Craft Beer™. Crafted with care, our beers are brewed with choicest malts; hopped with select hops to enhance your sensory pleasures, savoring our beers.
